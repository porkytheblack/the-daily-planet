EU passes landmark AI transparency act — 90 days to comply

The European Parliament voted 412-89 to enact the AI Transparency and Accountability Act, requiring all companies operating AI systems within the EU to disclose training data sources, model architectures, and downstream deployment contexts. The regulation takes effect in 90 days.

Editor's Note
This is the first regulation to mandate architectural disclosure, not just behavioral compliance. The second-order effects on open-source development are worth watching closely.
